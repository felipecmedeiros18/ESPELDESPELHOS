# **App Name**: Lumirror

## Core Features:

- Image Gallery: Showcase high-quality photos of LED mirrors in various settings (bathrooms, bedrooms, commercial spaces).
- Instagram Feed Integration: Embed a gallery with an Instagram feed for automatically updating photos and videos of mirrors.
- Contact Form via WhatsApp: Provide a prominent CTA button to request a quote via WhatsApp for personalized mirror designs.
- Product Customization Tool (AI-Powered): Implement an AI-driven tool to allow users to upload images of their spaces and visualize how different LED mirror designs would look, providing a realistic preview.
- Client Testimonials: Display customer testimonials and real-world photos to build trust and social proof.

## Style Guidelines:

- Primary color: Deep, saturated blue (#3257A8), reminiscent of the cool tones reflected in mirrors.
- Background color: Very light desaturated blue (#E8F0FE), almost white, to give a sense of space and cleanliness.
- Accent color: A lighter blue with a hint of green (#55A3A8), analogous to the primary but distinctive enough for interactive elements.
- Font: 'Inter', sans-serif, used for body and headline text for a professional and elegant look.
- Simple, elegant line icons to represent design, illumination, and sophistication, providing visual cues without clutter.
- Mobile-first responsive design with a focus on clear sections, ample spacing, and visual hierarchy to guide users effectively.
- Subtle hover animations on gallery images and smooth scroll effects to enhance the user experience without being distracting.
